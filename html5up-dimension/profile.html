A LITTLE ABOUT ME




GIRLS WHO CODE | July 2017- Current

I am currently a part of the Girls Who Code summer immersion program at BlackRock in New York City. It is a selective seven week coding-intensive program geared towards enabling young women to pursue education in computer science and other STEM fields. I've worked extensively with HTML/CSS to build and design websites. These past two months, I've also learned Python, JavaScript, Django/Bootstrap, Arduino, APIs, and object-oriented programming. The coolest part of Girls Who Code was gaining significant exposure to the intersection of technology and other industries including finance, law, and retail. It was awesome to talk with guest speakers, and be a part of interactive panels and on-site shadowing.

GWC HIGH SCHOOL | Mar 2014 - Jun 2017

I am currently taking classes at GWC High School for coding and web development. Web Developing had been an interest of mine for years now. I first found my passion for technology in middle school, when I started making basic HTML websites in my free time. I aspire to go to college for computer science becuase there aren't many women in the technology field. Therefore, I plan to thrive in my profession.
I think it's very important for girls to work together to empower each other so we can close the gender gap in technology-related sectors. Just because so much of technology is male-dominated, doesn't mean that we can't do it just as well. I hope to motivate younger girls to pursue computer science and enroll into GWC High.

BlackRock | FUture

If I decide to become a professional web designer, I would love to work at BlackRock. After spending 7 weeks with this company because my Girls Who Code summer immersion program was hosted in their headquarters, I learned a lot about the technological aspects of the finance sector. The Aladdin Product Group also seems very innovative and it would be great to be at the forefront of the intersection of these two industries with BlackRock!
