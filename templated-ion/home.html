Our Mission

We want to make communication between students easier by connecting them through a common social media platform for them to ask questions, meet others in their classes or school, and strengthen the community by connecting with other students all through an easy and accesible platform.

About Us

We are a group of five girls who have come together to create a social media platform that allows high school students to communicate with eachother enabling them to ask questions to other students in the school and their teachers. Our goal is to strengthen the community with in the school to create deeper relationships with students and teachers within their school.
