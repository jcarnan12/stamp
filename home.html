Mission Statement

We want to make communication between students easier by connecting through social media to ask questions and meet everyone in their school without having to have a face to face interaction.

A little more about us

We are a set of five girls that have came together to make a social media website to have teens in highschool to communicate with each other. This social media Website will enable teens from the same and different schools to communicate and make connections between different schools. Teens will be able to ask questions on homework, classes, and just be able to communicate with other teens that are in the same school as them. This will enable communication and support from classmates.
